Method: this dashboard creates a simple 24-hour reference price curve around the input reference price, then applies a symmetrical price sensitivity of ± the selected range. For each asset, annual energy is distributed across the 24 hours using a simplified technology shape. Fixed-price percentages are priced at the entered fixed level, while the remaining merchant share is exposed to hourly prices.
