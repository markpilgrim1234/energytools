This simplified annual model estimates the incremental value of adding a battery to a renewable asset. Renewable charging is limited to curtailed energy to isolate incremental value versus the renewable-only case. Grid charging is modeled as simple annual arbitrage using average charge and discharge prices. Battery LCOS includes battery CAPEX and OPEX, while grid charging costs are netted inside grid arbitrage revenue. Payback uses cumulative undiscounted incremental cash flows. The model does not optimize hourly dispatch, state of charge, negative prices, grid constraints by hour, taxes, debt repayment, or battery degradation.
