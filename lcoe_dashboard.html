LCOE Dashboard

LCOE (Levelized Cost of Energy) is a standard metric used to express the average cost of producing electricity over the life of an asset. This dashboard calculates LCOE based on installed capacity, year 1 yield, CAPEX, annual OPEX, capital structure, cost of debt and equity, annual degradation, OPEX inflation, and project life.

Formula: LCOE = PV(Costs) / PV(Energy)

Assumptions in this tool: CAPEX is treated at year 0, while annual energy and OPEX occur at year-end. Annual OPEX per MW escalates with the inflation input, no taxes are applied, and pre-tax WACC is computed as Debt % x Cost of Debt + Equity % x Cost of Equity. Yield is treated as MWh/MW for LCOE consistency.
