Battery Arbitrage Tool

A simple dispatch optimizer for a battery energy storage system. Use sliders for the most impactful battery variables and generate a 24-hour price curve from price-level and volatility presets. The tool estimates an economically rational hourly charge/discharge schedule, daily gross margin and annualized revenue.
